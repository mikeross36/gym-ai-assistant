### Gold's Gym International, Inc

- **Gold's Gym International, Inc.** – An American chain of international co-ed fitness centers (commonly referred to as gyms) originally started by Joe Gold in Venice Beach, California.
- **Gold's Gym History** – Joe Gold opened the first Gold's Gym in August 1965, in Venice Beach, California, long before the modern-day health club existed
- **Gold's Gym Corporate information** – Gold's Gym is privately owned by the international fitness company RSG Group and has about 68 company-owned gyms and via its franchising program about 520 international gyms
- **Gold's Gym Current operations** – Gold's Gym has locations across six continents. In 2015 and 2016, Gold's Gym topped the J.D. Power Health and Fitness Center Satisfaction Report.
- **Gold's Gym Corporate sales and wellness program** – The corporate sales and wellness program offers custom health and fitness plans for employees nationwide including memberships, nutrition, and wellness programs.
- **Gold's Gym Equipment in the studios** – Gold's Gyms all have a large strength training and free weight area with heavy dumbbells up to 150 kg, Olympic barbells, as well as an area for endurance training.
